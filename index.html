Spotify Popularity and Rolling Stone 500 Greatest Albums of All Time

Spotify ranking: I used Spotify's API to get each album's 'popularity' score (1 to 100; 100 is most popular). I then ordered the albums by popularity and assigned each a ranking of 1 to 852 (41 albums were not available) . According to Spotify, "Album popularity is derived mathematically from track popularity... The popularity of a track is a value between 0 and 100, with 100 being the most popular. The popularity is calculated by algorithm and is based, in the most part, on the total number of plays the track has had and how recent those plays are."
